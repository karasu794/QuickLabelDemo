### Key Features

![Checkmark icon](/api/content/dam/fedex-com/irc/apis/icon_checkmark.svg)

Validate domestic and international addresses

Cut down on delivery delays and additional service fees by confirming the validity of submitted addresses that are within supported countries.

![Calculator icon](/api/content/dam/fedex-com/irc/apis/icon_rates.svg)

Determine business and residential addresses

Increase the accuracy of rate quotes by determining if a submitted address is business or residential.
